# Malacath — The Cursed Tribe

## Quest Summary
Honor, exile, and brutality: help an outcast people survive (or exploit them).

## Recommended Act
Act II–III

## Key Beats
- Meet the Orc stronghold in crisis.
- Hunt the giant threat / retrieve a sacred item.
- Decide who leads and what “honor” means afterward.

## Choice Points
- Aid the outcasts vs manipulate them for war advantage.
- Embrace brutal honor vs try to civilize the conflict.

## Artifact (Extra)
**Volendrung:** Once per scene, spend a Fate point to create advantage **“Earth‑Shaking Blow”** with one free invoke (break gates, smash lines). GM compels collateral damage.

## Champion Mechanic
If the PC embraces the Prince’s path, add an aspect:
**Oath‑Breaker’s Doom (Malacath’s Hammer)**

## Daedric Influence
Tick **Malacath** up when the PC chooses vengeance or strength‑through‑hardship. Fill = grudges become active threats.

## GM Compels (Use Often)
- “Honor Demands Blood”
- “Outcast Sympathy”
- “Strength Isn’t Kind”
