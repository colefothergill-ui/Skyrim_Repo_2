# Creatures (Quick Fate Stat Blocks)

Use these as **scene-ready adversaries**. Adjust by fiction, not math.

## Draugr (Minion)
- Aspects: **“Ancient Nordic Dead”**, **“Bound to the Tomb”**
- Skills: Fight +1, Physique +1, Notice +1
- Stress: 1
- Stunt: **Relentless:** +2 to overcome when pushing forward in confined ruins.

## Draugr Deathlord (Elite)
- Aspects: **“Deathlord with a Name”**, **“Shout-Scarred”**
- Skills: Fight +4, Physique +3, Will +3, Notice +2
- Stress: 3, Consequence: Mild
- Stunt: **Unholy Command:** once per scene, create advantage **“Rise, Dead!”** with one free invoke.

## Thalmor Justiciar (Elite Social + Combat)
- Aspects: **“Diplomatic Immunity”**, **“Smiling Knife”**
- Skills: Deceit +4, Fight +3, Will +3, Notice +2, Rapport +2
- Stress: 2, Consequence: Mild
- Stunt: **Legal Threat:** +2 to create advantage when invoking law/treaties.

## Frost Troll (Brute)
- Aspects: **“Regenerating Monster”**, **“Territorial Hunger”**
- Skills: Physique +4, Fight +3, Athletics +2
- Stress: 3
- Stunt: **Regeneration:** first physical consequence each scene is reduced by 1 step unless fire is involved.
