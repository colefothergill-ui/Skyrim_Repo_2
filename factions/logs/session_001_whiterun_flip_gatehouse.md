# Session 001 — Whiterun Flips Without Burning

## Start Point
- Hold: Whiterun
- Location: Gatehouse / Dragonsreach / Bannered Mare district
- Situation: Balgruuf’s flip in progress; internal sabotage uncovered

## Key Events
- Eltric Stagborne secured parley via Offering of the Axe; Balgruuf agreed to conditional alignment with Stormcloaks.
- Proventus Avenicci attempted flight with Imperial runner; intercepted.
- Kyne’s Peace used to suppress civilian panic and prevent stampede.
- Thalmor handler “Sun-Wax” identified and eliminated; proof recovered (gold-wax seals).
- “Stablefire” fallback arson attempt prevented; torch extinguished; additional Thalmor proof recovered.
- Gatehouse confrontation: Legate Cipius detained after public duel + confession; ordered his men to stand down.
- Companions (Farkas, Aela) cooperated to secure horn + dead-drop and identify sympathizer network.

## Outcomes
- Whiterun civilian catastrophe prevented (no stablefire, no gatehouse massacre).
- Ulfric publicly swore restraint terms (no looting/slaughter; protect Temple of Kynareth; protect innocents).
- Eltric affirmed role as guarantor of Whiterun’s mercy (binding obligation).
- Proventus detained for public judgment by Balgruuf.
- Cipius detained; admitted Thalmor linkage and sabotage intent.

## Rumors / What the Hold Believes
- “The Stag shouted peace across the stones; even the dogs went quiet.”
- “Balgruuf chose Skyrim over the Empire—because the Empire chose the elves.”
- “Gold wax means Thalmor hands were on the knife.”
