# Boethiah — Boethiah’s Calling

## Quest Summary
A test of betrayal and ambition: Boethiah demands sacrifice and spectacle.

## Recommended Act
Act III

## Key Beats
- A cultist invites the PC to the proving ground.
- Boethiah demands a **betrayal** (symbolic or literal).
- Fight champions in a ritual arena.
- Claim the prize and the Prince’s attention.

## Choice Points
- Sacrifice an ally/important NPC vs refuse and anger the cult.
- Public domination vs subtle subversion.

## Artifact (Extra)
**Ebony Mail:** Once per scene, spend a Fate point to gain a scene aspect **“Poisoned Shadows”** with one free invoke; GM can compel “visibility of evil.”

## Champion Mechanic
If the PC embraces the Prince’s path, add an aspect:
**Boethiah’s Proving Blade**

## Daedric Influence
Tick **Boethiah** up on sacrifice or public domination. Fill = assassins and challengers arrive to “test” the PC.

## GM Compels (Use Often)
- “The Strong Deserve the World”
- “Betrayal as Currency”
- “Cultists Seek You”
